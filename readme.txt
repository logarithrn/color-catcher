# 🎮 Color Catcher

**Color Catcher** is a fast-paced arcade game where you move your basket to catch falling balls that match your color!  
Level up, collect power-ups, unlock achievements, and survive as long as you can!

---

## 🛠 How to Play
- Move the basket **left** or **right** with the **arrow keys** (or mobile buttons).
- Catch falling balls **that match your basket's color** to score points.
- **Avoid wrong-colored balls** — you lose a life if you catch the wrong color!
- **Collect power-ups**:
  - ❄️ Slow: Slow down all falling balls
  - ❤️ Life: Gain an extra life
  - ⭐ Double: Double your points for a short time
- **Pause the game** by pressing the `P` key.
- **Level up** every few points and watch the game speed increase!

## 🌎 How to Run Locally
1. Download and unzip the project folder.
2. Open `index.html` in any web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).
3. Play and enjoy!

---

## 📱 Mobile Support
- On mobile devices (phones, tablets), left/right buttons will appear at the bottom of the screen for easy tapping.
- No installation needed — just open in a mobile browser!
